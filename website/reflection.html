Group Reflection



As a group, the things that went well for us is that each member has an ability to keep their egos down to listen to others’ opinions. All members still retained a high sense of responsibility for their work. In this assignment, we had created a detailed plan for each part of the assignment. We learned many valuable lessons from the last assignment, so we decided to hold a meeting each week until there was no work that required a group decision. The plan and our tactic was really successful. The evidence for this is that we achieve the most ambitious goal in our, creating the finest prototype of our product in the given amount of time. We also divided specific roles for each member and created a tool to keep track with others' progress. With everything being planned carefully, the workflow of us became more smoothly, more effective and less stressful than the previous assignment, when we did not have a detailed plan to do our assignment.

Regarding the things that can be improved, we should aim for higher goals. We know that the prototype and the report have excellent results, thanks to all the team members. However, we really want to try to plan for a higher goal and see how far we can go in that path. Another thing that can be improved is that we can create a more detailed plan and make some changes in the plan due to the current stage in the week. We know that this might be time-consuming in some aspects, but with the changed plans for each stage, we can compare it to the original plan to see whether there are some things wrong that we need to fix. If we have that, each member will have more evidence to reflect back to the past and evolve more.

The surprising thing is that the atmosphere of the group and the effectiveness of the group is increasing time by time. At first, after understanding our group problem, we thought we can only work effectively as teammates, however we now like brothers that can feel very relaxing when sharing our opinions.

The most crucial lessons that we all learned from this experience is that each individual in the group is all very crucial. Each one also contributed to the group in their own ways. Even there are still some flaws, but we believe that it is unavoidable because each person has their own strengths and weaknesses. In conclusion, we are really happy to have a chance to work together.
